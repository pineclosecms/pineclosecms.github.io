What is this?

This is a Content Management System (CRM) used specifically for logging incidents regarding the tenant of 14 pine close.

It’s organized into categories for 𝗮-𝘇 Assault, Audio, Document, Harassment, Main Incidents, photos, police, video, WDH and more that can be found listed on the far right.

How to navigate

Accessing Content: all logged incidents on this platform are readily accessible with a click.

Navigation Menu: Located at the upper right corner is a navigation bar. The ‘All’ button displays a comprehensive list of all logged incidents, which you can easily browse. Adjacent to the ‘All’ button, a dropdown arrow that provides a selection menu, allowing you to look at incident reports from any page without the need to return to the index.

Content Filters: Next to the dropdown, you’ll find icons representing different media types, which serve as filters:

  • 📽️ Video Camera = Video
  • 🔊 Audio Speaker = Audio
  • 🖼️ Picture = Photos
  • 📄 Document = Documents


Viewing Modes: Beneath these icons is a toolbar featuring a toggle switch to alternate between Dark and Light viewing modes.

Sidebar Features: On the far right, the sidebar presents several options:
Recent Posts: displays the latest incidents, sorted by date, accessible from anywhere.
Categories: Displays the volume of content logged under each category. Selecting a category reveals a curated list of content within that category.
Tags: Each logged incident is assigned one or more tags, indicating the type of content included, such as documents, videos, and photos. Tags are visually scaled based on the quantity of associated content; selecting a tag filters the view to show only incidents with that content type.
Calendar: Allows for filtering of content by month. Selecting a date on the calendar will update the display to show content from that specific time frame.
